Favorite Bands

Pink Floyd

Started as a innovative psychedelic rock band in the late 60s, with Syd Barrett as the leader. Gradually incorporated other elements, waving between progressive rock, funk, pop, and lots of blues with new guitarist and vocalist David Gilmour.

Frank Zappa

Crazy, workaholic and innovative, has one of the most inspiring and complex discographies an artist can have. Despite not having a huge commercial success like other artists from his time, he's widely remembered by almost every important artist (such as The Beatles and Queen) as a huge inspiration. Until this day, is the only person include in both Rock and Jazz respective Halls of Fame.

Rush

One of the most prolific bands from Canada, they got international recognition after mixing Hard Rock with Progressive Rock in the 70s. They inspired generations of artists, such as Metallica, Dream Theather and pretty much every serious drummer in the world. Despite a huge and excellent discography, Moving Pictures was the album that made them one of the most followed bands around the world.
